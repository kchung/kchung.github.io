About

Hello, my name is Kevin Chung. I like to create nice things for websites. I am a front-end engineer at one of the best places to work, Weebly. There, my friends and I create a wonderful product to make the world a little nicer.

Born and raised in Reno, Nevada, I studied Computer Science and Engineering at the Unversity of Nevada, Reno. After graduating, I decided to venture towards the epicenter of tech and innovation, San Francisco.

I spend a lot of my free time mashing code and trying to learning new things, but on those off occasions away from the keyboard, I try to discover more reasons on what makes this city so great.

I also waited three years to purchase this domain.
